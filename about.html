About Book Explorer

Book Explorer is designed to help students and book lovers quickly find and save books.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the way people discover books. Users can browse popular books, search by title, and save their favorite books for easy access. We aim to create a fast, interactive, and student-friendly platform.

Key Features

  • Browse books fetched from the Open Library API
  • Search books by title
  • Add and remove books from Favorites (saved in localStorage)
  • Responsive pages for mobile, tablet, and desktop
  • Interactive UI with smooth user experience
